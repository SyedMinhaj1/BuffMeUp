Barbell Situp

    Foscued Muscules

  • ABS
  • Oblique
  • Shoulders

    Instruction
  1. Hold the bar with a slightly wider than shoulder width grip. Push the bar up toward the ceiling and maintain that elbow extension.
  2. Flex your abs and sit-up. Aim to get your torso vertical while maintaining the barbell overhead.

Dumbbell Russian Twist

    Foscued Muscules

  • ABS
  • Oblique
  • Back

    Instruction
  1. Sit on the floor and flex your knees and hips to a 90 degree angle.
  2. Your feet should be hovering off the ground. (If that's too hard start with heels on the floor)
  3. Rotate your upper spine to engage your obliques.

Barbell Roll Outs

    Foscued Muscules

  • ABS
  • Oblique
  • Shoulders

    Instruction
  1. Hold the Barbell with both hands and kneel on the floor with your feet up
  2. Slowly roll the Barbell straight forward, stretching your body into a straight position.
  3. After a pause at the stretched position, start pulling yourself back to the starting position. This should be a slow and controlled movement.

Hand Plank

    Foscued Muscules

  • ABS
  • Oblique
  • Shoulders
  • Back

    Instruction
  1. Start in a kneeling position with your hands planted on the ground.
  2. Pick your knees up off the ground. Hold in this position with a flat back.

Dumbbell Wood Chopper

    Foscued Muscules

  • Oblique
  • Back
  • ABS

    Instruction
  1. Stand with a shoulder width stance. Begin with the dumbbell near one of your quads.
  2. Rotate at your upper spine to engage the obliques.
  3. You can pivot your back foot to keep from generating too much torque at the knee.

Barbell Landmine Side Bend

    Foscued Muscules

  • Oblique
  • ABS
  • Back

    Instruction
  1. Stand straight and hold the end of the Barbell
  2. Keep your hips and shoulders facing forward, bend to one side slowly.
  3. Return to the upright position.
  4. Switch hands and repeat.

Cable Wood Chopper

    Foscued Muscules

  • Oblique
  • ABS
  • Back

    Instruction
  1. Set the cable at about nipple level. Use a handle attachment.
  2. Face sideways, then rotate your torso with your arms extended against the resistance.
  3. After completing your given number of reps, face the opposite direction and repeat.

Hand Side Plank

    Foscued Muscules

  • Oblique
  • ABS
  • Shoulders
  • Back

    Instruction
  1. Press your hand into the ground and pick up your hip off the ground.
  2. Open your chest and maintain this position for the allotted amount of time.
